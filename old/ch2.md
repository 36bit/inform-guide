Chapter 2: Setting Up Inform 6
==============================

Welcome to the exciting world of interactive fiction creation! This 
chapter is designed to guide you through the setup process of Inform 
6, your entry point to creating engaging text-based adventure games. 
By the end of this chapter, you will have a fully operational Inform 6 
environment ready to bring your interactive stories to life.

Familiarity with variables, data types, and control structures will 
help you grasp Inform 6's syntax and structure.

If you are new to programming, don't be discouraged! Inform 6 is 
designed to be accessible to beginners, and this guide, along with the 
supportive IF community, will provide ample resources to help you 
learn and succeed.

Downloading Inform 6: Acquiring the Building Blocks
---------------------------------------------------

Inform 6 is readily available for download on various platforms:

-   **GNU/Linux**: Many GNU/Linux distributions offer pre-compiled
    Inform 6 packages through their package managers. Pre-compiled
    packages are often the most straightforward and most convenient
    installation method. Check with your distribution.
-   **Windows**: Download the pre-compiled Inform 6 executable from
    the Interactive Fiction Archive:
    <https://www.ifarchive.org/if-archive/infocom/compilers/inform6/executables/>
-   **macOS**:
    -   First install Homebrew: <https://brew.sh/>
    -   Install Inform 6 using Homebrew:
        -   Open a terminal window.
        -   Type the command brew install inform6 and press Enter.

Compiling Inform 6 from Source: Building from the Ground Up
-----------------------------------------------------------

Compiling from source allows you to access the latest version and bug 
fixes that may not be available in pre-compiled versions.

1.  **Obtain the Source Code**:

You can download the Inform 6 source code from the following
repositories:

-   David Kinder's Inform 6 compiler source code:
    <https://github.com/DavidKinder/Inform6>
-   David Griffith's source code package for Unix-like systems:
    <https://gitlab.com/DavidGriffith/inform6unix>

2.  **Install Dependencies**:

Compiling Inform 6 requires a C compiler.

3.  **Build**:

Once you have downloaded the source code and installed the necessary
dependencies, follow the instructions in the README.

Verifying Installation: Testing the Scaffolding
-----------------------------------------------

After installation, it is essential to verify that Inform 6 functions
correctly. Here's how:

-   Open a terminal interface (or use the command prompt on Windows).
-   Type the command inform -v and press Enter.

If Inform 6 is installed correctly, you will see information about the
compiler version displayed on the screen.

Choosing an Interpreter: Opening the Gateway to Your Worlds
-----------------------------------------------------------

You need an interpreter program to play the interactive fiction games
you create with Inform 6. Several interpreters are available, each
with its features and capabilities. Frotz is a widely available and
well-established interpreter that supports a broad range of Z-machine
versions, but others also exist.

The choice of interpreter depends on your specific needs and
preferences.

Troubleshooting: Overcoming Obstacles on the Path
-------------------------------------------------

While setting up Inform 6 is generally straightforward, you may 
encounter some common issues. Here are some troubleshooting tips:

-   **Compiler Not Found**: Ensure Inform 6 is installed in a
    directory in your system's PATH environment variable. This allows
    you to run the inform command from any directory.
-   **Compilation Errors**: Carefully review the error messages
    generated by Inform 6. These messages often provide specific
    information about the problem and how to fix it.

If you encounter any issues not listed above, don't hesitate to seek
help from the Inform 6 community. Online forums and discussion groups
are valuable resources for troubleshooting and obtaining assistance
from experienced IF creators.

With Inform 6 successfully installed and ready for use, you can embark
on your interactive fiction development journey. In the following
chapters, we will explore the Inform 6 language and its capabilities
in detail, providing you with the knowledge and tools to create your
captivating textual worlds.
